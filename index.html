About our project

Our goal was to determine what the single most impactful factor was on whether or not a person has heart disease. To do this, each one of us found a dataset and, after analyzing them seperately, we merged three of the datasets and analyzed them as a whole. Shababa's dataset was unable to be merged into the other three and therefor we analyzed it as a seperate dataset.
